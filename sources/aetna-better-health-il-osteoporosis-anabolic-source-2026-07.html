Step Therapy

This means you may need to try certain drugs first to treat your condition. After the first drug is tried, the plan will then cover the other drug for that same condition. For example, Drug A and Drug B may treat your condition. The plan may not cover Drug B unless you try Drug A first. If Drug A does not work for you, then Drug B will be covered.  For a complete list of all drugs requiring step therapy, please review your formulary guide.
