Position Statements

Addressing clinical issues pertinent to women at midlife and beyond. 

Position Statements

Position Statements from The Menopause Society address important and up-to-date clinical issues pertinent to women at midlife and beyond. They are developed by an expert Advisory Panel and published in The Society’s journal, Menopause. 

Each Position Statement includes continuing education credits available to members of The Menopause Society. Members also have access to accompanying PowerPoint slides summarizing the paper’s contents. There are also Patient Handouts on the topics. Statements are available for viewing and downloading.
