Prescription Drug Lists for Medicaid Plans

You can download printable formularies (prescription drug lists) for Medicaid plans in PDF format using the links below. You can also use our online search tool for the most up-to-date information.

DMAS has obtained authority from CMS to allow 90-day prescriptions to continue past the date of the public health emergency. As of October 1, 2021, drugs posted at Virginia DMAS 90 Day Supply List (PDF) may be covered for 90 days.
